01.

It parses the mess.

Numbered, lettered, footnoted answer keys, scanned pages — bring the worst documents you have. The parser was trained on what your teacher actually hands out, not on perfectly-formatted PDFs.

02.

It grades itself.

Difficulty isn't a vibe. Every question is scored against Bloom's taxonomy — recall, application, analysis — so you study what's hard, not just what's first.

03.

It explains.

"The answer is C" is useless at 2am. Every answer ships with a one-line why, traced back to the exact sentence in your source.
